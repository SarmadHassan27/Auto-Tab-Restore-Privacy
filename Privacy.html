Privacy Policy

Auto Tab Restore does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or sensitive user data. The extension operates entirely on your device and uses Chrome’s local storage API to save tab information for restoration purposes.

No data is sent to any server or third party. All saved data stays within your browser and is only used to reopen your tabs when Chrome starts.

We respect your privacy — there are no trackers, analytics, or hidden processes in this extension.
